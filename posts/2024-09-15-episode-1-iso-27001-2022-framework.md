In this episode, we dive into the world of information security standards, specifically ISO 27001 and 27002. Our hosts break down the 2022 updates to these crucial guidelines, exploring how they shape modern approaches to protecting data and systems. From the importance of risk management to the often-overlooked human factor in security, we cover it all. Whether you're a tech professional or simply interested in safeguarding your personal information, this podcast offers valuable insights into the evolving landscape of cybersecurity. Join us as we demystify complex concepts and discuss practical strategies for creating a robust information security management system in today's digital age.
